Vrindra Store wants to create an annual sales report for 2023.So, that, Vrindra can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2024.

After Analysing the data, 
Sample Insights : 
Women are more likely to buy compared to Men (~65%)
Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3
Adult age group (30-40 yrs) is max contributing (~50%)
Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels are max contributing

And the final conclusion to Improve Vrinda Sales:

Target Women customers of age group (30-39 yrs) living in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra
